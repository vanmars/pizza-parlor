About Mamma Marjia's



A humble street cafe

Mamma Marija's started on a small narrow street as a pizza parlor. Marija wanted to help feed her brothers, who labored all day on the sea as fisherman and were often quite hungry by the time they came off their boats. With her secret sauces and top-notch topping combinations, Marija ended up not only feeding her brothers, but also most of the town. With the success of her street cafe, Marija was able to move her restaurant to its current prime location on the harbor, giving guest not only great cuisine but also great views.
